About the Photographer

Cailin Stone is a skilled photographer who specializes in wildlife and landscape photography. Stone began her photography career at the young age of nine with the teachings of her father. Since then, she has taken numerous courses in photography and digital editing. She shoots photos primarily on a Sony Alpha 6400 digital camera. With years of experience in the field, her photos are guaranteed to be stunning and impactful. She currently attends the University of Oregon where she continues to pursue her passion for photography.
